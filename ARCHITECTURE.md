# ARCHITECTURE.md

This document captures the early structural direction established in Phase 0.
It is intentionally lightweight and should be expanded in Phase 2 when sitemap, navigation, CTA mapping, and footer structure are defined formally.

## Core Page Layout Direction

### Home

- large logo-led hero
- clear one-sentence organisation introduction
- early split between community and elite pathways
- founder or mission credibility block
- current activity or social proof block
- direct enquiry pathway near the end of the page

### About

- opener with mission and brand truth
- founder story or origin section drawn from the PDF narrative
- values or philosophy section
- credibility block that explains lived football experience
- simple closing CTA to contact or explore programs

### Programs

- concise hero that names the program offering clearly
- scan-friendly cards or rows for major offer groups
- short explainer on who each option is for
- one consistent CTA pattern for enquiry or expression of interest

### Events or Current Activity

- visual lead section for featured activity
- short editorial-style summary blocks instead of dense calendar tooling
- support for Instagram or recent media references
- CTA to enquire about attendance, collaboration, or future events

### Contact

- plain-language explanation of what the form is for
- minimal enquiry form with only necessary fields
- supporting email and social contact options
- clear follow-up expectation copy

## Shared Layout Rules

- Every page should open with immediate orientation before storytelling expands the context.
- Section counts should stay restrained so mobile rhythm remains calm and readable.
- Calls to action should repeat consistently, but only at meaningful decision points.
- Story-led sections should use editorial pacing and generous spacing instead of dashboard-like density.
- Logo, colour, and atmosphere may shift by approved theme, but page structure should remain stable across themes.
- Early implementation may begin from a green-led baseline, but the structural docs should not be treated as locking the eventual Phase 3 palette to green.
