# PHASE_2_INFORMATION_ARCHITECTURE.md

This document records the iterative outputs for Phase 2 in the exact order defined in `ROADMAP.md`.
It expands the structural baseline from `ARCHITECTURE.md`, preserves the audience and content rules from `PHASE_1_FOUNDATION.md` and `CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`, and keeps `assets/YARD.pdf` as the canonical source for meaning and narrative direction.

## 2.1 Sitemap

### Sitemap Principles

- The first release sitemap must stay small, clear, and mobile-friendly.
- Community and elite pathways should be easy to find without fragmenting the site into too many top-level destinations.
- Core navigation should prioritise first-visit orientation, trust, and enquiry.
- Future expansion should be anticipated without promoting future pages into the primary v1 structure too early.

### Primary Sitemap for v1

1. `Home`
2. `About`
3. `Programs`
4. `Events`
5. `Contact`

### Nested Structure Within the v1 Sitemap

- `Programs`
  - community football experiences
  - elite pathway development
  - workshops and education
  - events and gala days
  - all-abilities and inclusive football
  - storytelling, art, and creative projects

### Relationship Rules

- `Home` should orient visitors and route them to the right next step.
- `About` should carry the founder story, mission, values, and organisational truth.
- `Programs` should act as the main structured overview of what Yard offers across both community and elite contexts.
- `Events` should handle current activity, featured sessions, gala days, and editorial updates without becoming a dense calendar product.
- `Contact` should remain the dedicated conversion page for email enquiry and expression of interest.

### Future Sitemap Reserve

The following pages may be added later when content depth and operational need justify them:

- `Partnerships`
- `Player Stories`
- `Gallery / Media`
- `Blog / Journal`
- `Camps / Clinics` landing pages

These remain future expansion paths and should not be elevated into the Phase 2 v1 sitemap yet.
