Purpose

Backyard honesty, professional delivery, real impact in sport.

YARD exists to bring football back to purpose: confidence, belonging, health, and growth. We partner with clubs, schools, and organisations to create practical programs and events that move girls' sport forward.

Focus Areas

Education, wellbeing, leadership, and culture built for modern football communities.

Performance Education

Menstrual-cycle awareness, athlete development, and practical performance guidance with specialist contributors such as Christie Tadros.

Girls in Sport Research

Presentation-led research translation for clubs and families, turning current evidence into clear, usable action.

Mental Health and Wellbeing

Workshops and courses that support players, coaches, and parents with healthy, sustainable sporting habits.

Daddy & Girl Program

A program exploring positive male influence in a girl's football pathway, informed by lived experience and research.

Collabs and Community

Creative collaborations and launch events with clubs, schools, and sporting organisations to scale impact.

Film and Memory Sessions

Intimate football film discussions and story circles to reconnect people with formative sporting memories and future purpose.
